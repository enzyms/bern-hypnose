# If your Google Business Profile isn’t showing on desktop but appears on mobile, it’s likely due to optimization or signal issues. Ensure your GBP links to a dedicated location landing page optimized for the specific city where the GBP is located, not the homepage, to avoid mixed signals . Check NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across all citations and ensure your GBP category matches your primary service. Also, update your GBP with fresh photos, posts, and reviews to boost engagement signals. If the issue persists, test from different desktop locations using a VPN or Google’s Ad Preview Tool to identify if it’s location-specific.
